About Chadashiel Education

Chadashiel Education is a dedicated platform for students preparing for the SAT. I was once very bad at both Math and English, scoring 55.5% in my secondary school math entrance exam and scoring around a 550 in the SAT EBRW section during my first test. However, with an intensive class teaching me Math for 4 straight weeks, I was able to score a perfect 800 in the SAT Math section at age 14 on my first attempt, and I was able to build my English skills to score a 720 in the SAT EBRW section after weeks of study. I believe that what is now as a one-man start-up will grow into a community of learners and educators. My mission is to provide support, advice, and high-quality resources to help students achieve their best and desired scores. I believe and know that with the right guidance and resources, every student can succeed in their SAT journey.
